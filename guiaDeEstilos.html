Colors

This page will have as principal color and more important the black RGB(51,51,51) and the secondary will be the orange RGB(255,140,0.)
The principal color will be using for headers and the secondary for the footers of the pages. We have to remember that the RGB(255,255,255) will be used for the texts of the headers because of
the background, but in the rest we will use the black RGB(0,0,0).

Typography

For H1 tags the text will have a size of 75px, the font will be 'Dancing Script' italic and with a margin of 30px same as in the rest of the elements that I mention now.
For the rest of the tags, including paragraphs, we will use a 'Open Sans' typeface, sans-serif and with 30px side margins for all text included in the body.
Regarding the size of the text, it will be 25px in the case of H2, 20px for H3 and for the paragraphs 16px.
